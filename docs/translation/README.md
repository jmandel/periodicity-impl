### Localization & Translations

> [!NOTE]
> Currently, only the mobile app is translatable. All files referenced in these guides can be found in the `/app` directory.

Our project is translated by volunteers on Crowdin.

* **If you are a translator,** please see our [**Translators Guide**](TRANSLATORS.md) for instructions on how to join and contribute.
* **If you are a developer,** you must follow our [**Developers Guide**](DEVELOPERS.md) for adding new text to the app.
* **If you are a maintainer,** you must follow our [**Maintainers Guide**](MAINTAINERS.md) for full app support.
