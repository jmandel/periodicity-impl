Menstrudel puts the implementation in Data Management as a Cycle FHIR share panel. The flow starts with the local profile scope, a date range, and independent toggles for the core bleeding facts, flow categories, symptoms, and the app's menstrual pain level enum. Identity is intentionally not included; the local profile is enough for the app but not enough to emit a Patient resource.

The preview is the contract. It is built from the same in-memory snapshot that later becomes the FHIR collection Bundle, so counts, omissions, and the encrypted payload cannot drift. Menstrudel maps logged bleeding days, explicit non-bleeding days, flow, symptom rows, and app-native pain levels. Predictions, period summaries, contraception, sexual activity, sanitary product details, and missing days are left out.

Create live QR encrypts the Bundle locally and sends only compact JWE ciphertext to shlep, with a seven-day expiry and five-open limit. The copy, share, and open buttons reuse the exact viewer-prefixed link, and Stop deletes the live share.
