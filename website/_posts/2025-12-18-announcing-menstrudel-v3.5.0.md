We're excited to release version **3.5.0** of Menstrudel. This update brings **Logging reminders** and several fixes.

## Get the Menstrudel App

- 🤖 [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.whitticase.menstrudel)
- 🍎 [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/menstrudel/id6751970511)


### What's New
* **Logging Reminders** Receive a reminder to log the following day after flow has been logged.

* **More Translations** Thanks to the community, the app now has better translation support!

<h3 style="text-align: center;">Logging Reminders</h3>
![Logging Reminders](/assets/images/blog/2025-12-18-announcing-menstrudel-v3.5.0/logging_reminders.gif)

When you log your flow, the app can now automatically schedule a gentle nudge for the following day to remind you to keep your logs updated. This helps maintain a complete and accurate history of your period without you having to remember to open the app.

Control your notifications: This feature is disabled by default. You can turn it on by navigating to Settings -> Logging -> Enable Logging Reminders.
