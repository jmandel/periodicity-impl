We are excited to announce **Menstrudel v4.2.0**. This update introduces smarter symptom sorting and addresses several reported bugs.

## Get the Menstrudel App

- 🤖 [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.whitticase.menstrudel)
- 🍎 [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/menstrudel/id6751970511)


### What’s New

* **Age Ordered Symptoms:** Symptoms are now ordered by age relevance.

---

<h3 style="text-align: center;">Age Ordered Symptoms</h3>

Symptoms are now ordered based on age relevance following research by **Cunningham et al. (2024)**. 

* **How it works:** Enter your date of birth in 'User' settings, and the app will prioritise symptoms most relevant to your age.
* **Note:** No symptoms are hidden; custom symptoms will always appear at the bottom of the list.
