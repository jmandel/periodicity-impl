We're excited to release version **3.4.0** of Menstrudel. This update brings **Improved Sanitary Product Tracking**, **More Insights** and several fixes.

## Get the Menstrudel App

- 🤖 [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.whitticase.menstrudel)
- 🍎 [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/menstrudel/id6751970511)


### What's New
* **Improved Sanitary Product Tracking** Introducing a dedicated, brand-new screen for sanitary product tracking (e.g., pads, tampons, cups). Effortlessly log usage for a more complete and accurate picture of your flow.

* **Improved Insights** The Insights screen has had some love. With new overall logging stats and carousel design.

* **More Translations** Thanks to the community, the app now has better translation support!

<h3 style="text-align: center;">Sanitary Product Tracking</h3>
![Sanitary Product Tracking](/assets/images/blog/2025-12-08-announcing-menstrudel-v3.4.0/sanitary_product_tracking.gif)

A new dedicated screen for tracking sanitary products. With new products to track and an improved notification system!

<h3 style="text-align: center;">Improved Insights</h3>
![Improved Insights Screen](/assets/images/blog/2025-12-08-announcing-menstrudel-v3.4.0/insights_screen.gif)

We've made small, effective tweaks to the **Insights** screen to better utilise space and display metrics at a glance.

**Total logged days** and the **full span of your tracking history** values have been added. This helps you understand your data depth and consistency immediately.

## Get Involved

We love hearing from you! Menstrudel is an open-source project, and its success is a team effort. Here’s how you can help us make it even better:

* **Share Your Thoughts:** Found a **bug**? Have an idea for a **new feature**? We track all development on **GitHub**.
    * **Open a Discussion:** [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/J-shw/Menstrudel/discussions) is the best place to share general feedback and ideas.
    * **Report an Issue:** If you find a bug, please [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/J-shw/Menstrudel/issues) to help us fix it quickly.
    * **Contribute Code:** If you're a developer, check out the source code and [submit a Pull Request](https://github.com/J-shw/Menstrudel).

* **Help with Translations:** Is there a language *you* want supported? Join our effort and help us translate Menstrudel on **Crowdin**: [https://crowdin.com/project/menstrudel](https://crowdin.com/project/menstrudel)

* **Spread the Word:** Tell your friends about Menstrudel! Leaving a **5-star review** on the App Store or Google Play is one of the easiest ways to support the app.
