Privacy by Default

Your health data isn't a commodity. Menstrudel stores everything locally on your device, ensuring your most personal information stays exactly where it belongs: with you.

  • Zero Cloud: Your data never leaves your phone. No accounts, no servers.
  • 100% Offline: Full functionality without an internet connection.
  • Open Source: Transparent, community-driven, and free forever.
